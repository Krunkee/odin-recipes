Lasagna Recipe

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this easy lasagna recipe:

Beef: This easy lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey for a lighter option. Spaghetti sauce: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce. Cheeses: You'll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Eggs: Eggs help bind the cheese mixture together. Plus, they lend moisture and richness. Seasonings: Season the easy lasagna with dried parsley, salt, and black pepper. Lasagna noodles: Of course, you'll need lasagna noodles!
